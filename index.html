DogePumpFan - White Paper

1. Introduction

DogePumpFan is a token designed to stabilize the Dogecoin market through innovative mechanisms that promote organic growth, staking, and reduced volatility. Built on the Solana blockchain, DogePumpFan combines the speed of transactions and cost efficiency with a well-structured economy to deliver tangible benefits to the Dogecoin community.

2. The Problem

Dogecoin faces several challenges:

  • High volatility.
  • Lack of staking mechanisms for passive income.
  • Limited integration with modern blockchain ecosystems.

3. The DogePumpFan Solution

DogePumpFan addresses these issues by:

  • Offering staking on the Solana blockchain.
  • Implementing an economic model that encourages long-term holding.
  • Integrating seamlessly into modern blockchain ecosystems.

4. Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1 billion tokens.
  • Distribution:
    • 30% - Presale.
    • 20% - Liquidity on Raydium.
    • 20% - Airdrop.
    • 15% - Staking rewards.
    • 10% - Team and development.
    • 5% - Marketing and partnerships.
  • Launch Price: 0.01 SOL/token.
  • Minimum Buy-in: 1 SOL (100 tokens).

5. Roadmap

  • Q1 2025: Launch presale and airdrop.
  • Q2 2025: Launch staking and list on Raydium.
  • Q3 2025: Expand partnerships and integrations.
  • Q4 2025: Introduce a DAO mechanism.
